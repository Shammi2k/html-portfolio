The Best Movies According to Shammi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

One of the best movies I have ever watched, especially being someone who has gone through the engineering phase.

Interstellar

I love sci-fi movies, but Interstellar took it to another mindboggling level. To understand this movie, you will be needing multiple watches and a whole lot of popcorn.

Shutter Island

If you want a mystery that will take you some time to digest, this is the movie for you.
